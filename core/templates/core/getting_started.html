Getting Started with WMS

Learn how to organize your personal belongings with WMS. This guide will walk you through the core concepts and features.

What WMS Solves

Personal storage comes with two major pain points that WMS addresses:

Storage Unit Transparency

Ever forget what's inside a bin, locker, or storage box? WMS provides a simple digital inventory so you always know what's in each storage unit.

Item Location

Can't find your red jacket or holiday decorations? WMS uses AI-powered search to help you locate items using natural language queries like "where is my winter coat?"

Core Concepts

WMS organizes your belongings using a simple hierarchy:

Location

A physical place like your home, office, or storage facility. Locations are organizational containers—they hold Units but not Items directly.

Example: "My House", "Office Building", "Mom's Garage"

Unit

A physical container that holds your Items. Units can exist inside Locations, or be nested inside other Units for complex organization.

Example: "Blue Storage Bin", "Garage Shelf", "Closet Drawer"

Item

Your actual belongings! Items always live inside a Unit. Each item has a name, description, and optional photo for easy identification.

Example: "Red Winter Jacket", "Holiday Lights", "Power Drill"

Example Hierarchy:

My House → Garage Shelf → Blue Bin → Holiday Lights

Creating Storage Spaces

Start by creating the storage spaces where your items live:

  1. Name your space — Give it a memorable name like "Garage" or "Hall Closet"
  2. Choose the type — Select whether it's a Location (organizational) or a Unit (holds items)
  3. Add details (optional) — Include a description, address (for Locations), or dimensions (for Units)
  4. Nest if needed — Place Units inside Locations or other Units to mirror your real organization

Adding Items

WMS makes adding items quick and easy with AI-powered auto-fill:

  1. Take a photo — Snap a picture of your item, or upload an existing image
  2. AI auto-fills details — Our AI analyzes the image and suggests a name and description
  3. Review and confirm — Edit the suggested details if needed, then save
  4. Or skip the photo — Enter item details manually if you prefer

Tip: Better photos lead to better AI suggestions! Try to capture the item clearly, entirely, singularly and with good lighting.

Finding Items with AI Search

When you need to find something, just ask in plain English:

"Where is my red jacket?"
"Find the holiday decorations"
"I need my power tools"

WMS uses AI to understand your query and match it against your item descriptions and images. It will tell you exactly which Unit contains your item.

QR Codes for Quick Access

Every Unit gets a unique QR code. Print it and attach it to your physical storage container for instant access:

  1. Download the QR code — From any Unit's detail page, download its unique QR code
  2. Print and attach — Print the QR code and stick it on your physical bin, box, or shelf
  3. Scan anytime — Use your phone's camera to scan the QR code
  4. Instant inventory — You'll be taken directly to that Unit's contents in WMS

Tip: Laminate your QR codes if they will be exposed to weather.
